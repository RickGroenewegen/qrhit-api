This Privacy Policy explains how Music Match, published by Source 1 Media B.V., handles personal information. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring a safe experience for all users, including children under the age of 16.

1. About Music Match

Music Match is a companion app for the Music Match game. Users can scan Music Match playing cards, and the app will open the corresponding song in Spotify for gameplay purposes.

2. Data Collection

Music Match does not collect, store, or process any personal data.

  • No accounts are required.
  • No names, email addresses, payment information, or location data are collected.
  • No cookies, analytics, or tracking technologies are used.

3. Children's Privacy

Music Match is designed to be safe for all ages, including children under 16. Because no personal data is collected, there is no risk of misuse of children's information.

4. Third-Party Services

The app uses Spotify to play songs. When users are redirected to Spotify, they are subject to Spotify's own terms and privacy policy. We encourage users to review Spotify's Privacy Policy.

5. Data Sharing

We do not share any personal data with third parties, since no data is collected or stored.

6. International Use

Music Match is available worldwide. Regardless of the country of use, this policy applies equally to all users.

7. User Rights

Since no personal data is collected or stored, users do not need to request access, correction, or deletion of data. If you have questions about privacy, you may still contact us.
